About Us

📍 Location & History

  1. Located in Krishnankovil, Virudhunagar District, Tamil Nadu
  2. Founded in 1984 as Arulmigu Kalasalingam College of Engineering by philanthropist Thiru T. Kalasalingam
  3. Became a deemed-to-be university in 2006, recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC)

🎓 Academics

  1. Offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across multiple disciplines:
    • Engineering & Technology (B.Tech, M.Tech, Ph.D.)
    • Science (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Computer Science)
    • Management Studies (MBA, Ph.D.)
    • Humanities & Social Sciences
    • Architecture
  2. Comprises 12 schools with specialized departments
  3. Accredited by NAAC (A++ grade), NBA, and ABET

🌟 Rankings & Recognition

  1. Ranked #36 in Engineering (NIRF 2025)
  2. Featured in Times Higher Education (THE) Impact Rankings 2025
  3. Recognized for research excellence, innovation, and sustainability initiatives

🏫 Campus & Facilities

  1. Spread across a green, eco-friendly campus with modern infrastructure
  2. Facilities include:
    • Central library with digital resources
    • Advanced laboratories
    • Hostels for boys and girls
    • Sports complexes and recreational areas
    • Focus on renewable energy and sustainability projects

🌍 International Collaboration

  1. Partnerships with universities abroad for student exchange programs
  2. Opportunities for joint research and global exposure

💼 Placements & Career Support

  1. Strong industry connections with companies like TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Cognizant, Accenture, and core engineering firms
  2. Dedicated placement cell offering:
    • Training in soft skills and technical skills
    • Internship opportunities
    • Career guidance and counseling

🎯 Key Highlights

  1. Inclusive education: Special support for differently-abled students
  2. Research-driven: Encourages innovation and entrepreneurship
  3. Community focus: Engages in rural development and social responsibility projects

👉 In essence:

KARE is a premier institution in Tamil Nadu, blending academic excellence, research, global exposure, and strong placement opportunities.

We offer diverse programs, world-class faculty, and a vibrant campus life that fosters growth and creativity.
